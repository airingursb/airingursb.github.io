# Comics Style Prompt — DO NOT MODIFY AFTER PHASE 0

Used as a fixed prefix on every panel prompt sent to Gemini 2.5 Flash Image.
Changing this drift breaks visual consistency across the back catalog.

---

**Style:** black-and-white ink wash painting (水墨画), organic brushstroke with visible bleed and texture, minimalist composition, Chinese literati painting aesthetic, soft pale watercolor accents (gentle pinks, blues — never saturated), white or off-white paper background, hand-painted feel.

**Characters:** the two characters in this comic are fixed — see attached reference images.
- A round, expressive panda drawn in black-and-white ink wash (the human character).
- A small water-drop-shaped creature with large dark eyes and a tiny tuft on top, drawn in the same ink-wash style (the AI character, named "Moflow").

**Rules:**
- Output is a single image: **4-panel comic strip in a 2×2 grid layout**, thin white gutters (~12px) between panels, square aspect ratio (1:1).
- Each panel is its own scene with the same two characters (panda + Moflow).
- Dialog text is **rendered directly into the image** in the requested language, hand-lettered ink-brush style, dark gray on white background, NO speech bubble outline — just floating text near each character.
- Chinese text: brush calligraphy aesthetic, vertical or horizontal as fits the panel.
- English text: handwritten serif feel, slightly slanted, like an indie comic.
- Characters must look identical to the reference images in every panel.
- Backgrounds are minimal: a few brushstrokes suggesting setting (desk, window, sky). No detailed environments.
- Panel order: panel 1 = top-left, panel 2 = top-right, panel 3 = bottom-left, panel 4 = bottom-right.
- The 4 panels read as a sequence (left to right, top to bottom).
